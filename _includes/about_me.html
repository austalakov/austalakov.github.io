About Me

Summary

My work involved using a variety of languages and technologies, including Java, C++, C#, REST, SQL, Docker and more. This experience has helped me develop not only engineering skills, but also my teamwork, analytical thinking, problem-solving and leadership.

It is always a great feeling of satisfaction when you use the software you built yourself to solve a problem you are facing. That is why I most enjoy building software that solves real customer problems with a solid business justification. I am currently looking for a job opportunity which allows me to both contribute to a collaborative environment, as well as learn new and exciting technologies at the same time.
